About us

Welcome to DRT Services Pvt Ltd

certified International Recruitment Manpower Agency

DRT Services Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001:2018 certified International Recruitment Manpower agency based in Kolhapur Maharashtra. DRT Services Pvt Ltd has been recognized as a trusted recruiter in the space of Shutdown Projects besides Onshore, Offshore & Long term Projects. We specialize in EPC Projects , Upstream & Downstream Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Structural Fabrication, Substation and Transmission line Construction, Civil & Electro-Mechanical Construction, Facility maintenance, FMCG, Hospitality, Automobile, Aluminium Plants and other miscellaneous services. We also have a service of deputing Technical Manpower for outsourcing and contract staff.

DRT Services Pvt Ltd pan-India associates for specific categories of manpower with sub Govt. approved Trade centres to conduct trade test and technical assessment for shortlisted candidates.

Our Team of professionally qualified &amp; technically sound Engineers and coordinators help in targeting and mobilizing the right candidates with the relevant skills to ensure deadlines are met. We maintain a healthy data bank of various categories and keep refining the same regularly during every batch of mobilization through advertisements in leading newspapers as well as headhunting.

DRT Services Pvt Ltd stands apart when it comes to our sourcing skills, where our shortlisting of candidates are among the best in the Industry and are almost the perfect match for our Clients.

Teamwork, Innovation and Client satisfaction is the core that drives our working environment.

Our History

Our History Moving ahead, one step at a time!

NeptuneInc, commenced operations in 2018 as International Recruitment Agency in Kolhapur Maharashtra, India. Today, from humble beginnings, we have grown into a formidable player against the big names in the Industry with a reputation for speed of delivery and quality of service. In a short period of time, we have earned the respect and trust of prestigious companies across the Arabian Gulf ,Europe , Africa etc as your Trusted Placement partner,catering to your manpower needs of diverse verticals across the world when it comes to their manpower requirements from India.

DRT Services Pvt Ltd has been recognized as a trusted recruiter in the space of Shutdown Projects besides Onshore, Offshore & Long term Projects. We also specialize in sourcing manpower for EPC Projects, Upstream & Downstream Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Structural Fabrication, Substation and Transmission line Construction, Civil & Electro-Mechanical Construction, Aluminum Plants, Facility maintenance, FMCG, Hospitality, Automobile among other services.

The strength of DRT Services Pvt Ltd lies in the open interaction between our Clients and candidates. All our clients rated our service much better than our competitors.
